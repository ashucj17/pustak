About Pustak

Where stories come alive and knowledge finds its home. Discover the heart and soul behind India's beloved bookstore that has been nurturing minds and fostering the love of reading for generations.

Our Story

Founded with a simple belief that books have the power to transform lives, Pustak has grown from a small neighborhood bookstore to a cultural institution that celebrates literature, learning, and community.

Our Heritage

Established in 1985, Pustak began as a dream to create a space where book lovers could discover, explore, and connect. Over the decades, we've remained committed to our founding principles while evolving with the times.

Community Focus

More than just a bookstore, Pustak serves as a cultural hub where readers gather, authors meet their audience, and literary discussions flourish. We believe in building bridges through books.

Diverse Collection

From classic literature to contemporary fiction, academic texts to children's picture books, our carefully curated collection spans genres, languages, and cultures to serve every reading preference.

Our History

A journey through four decades of literary excellence, community building, and unwavering commitment to the power of books.

1985-1995

The Beginning

Founded by Rajesh Sharma with just 500 books and a dream, Pustak opened its doors in a small 200 sq ft space in Kanpur. The focus was on serving the local academic community with textbooks and reference materials.

1995-2005

Growth & Expansion

As our reputation grew, we expanded our collection to include fiction, poetry, and children's books. We moved to our current location and began hosting our first author readings and book club meetings.

2005-Present

Digital Integration

Embracing the digital age while maintaining our physical presence, we launched our online platform in 2010 and have since become a bridge between traditional and modern bookselling approaches.

Our Mission & Values

We are driven by core values that shape every aspect of our work and service to the community.

Accessibility

Books for Everyone

We believe that access to books and knowledge should be universal. Our pricing, programs, and outreach initiatives ensure that financial barriers never prevent someone from discovering their next great read.

Quality

Thoughtful Curation

Every book on our shelves is selected with care. Our team of literary enthusiasts ensures that we offer not just popular titles, but also hidden gems and emerging voices worth discovering.

Innovation

Embracing the Future

While honoring traditional bookselling values, we embrace technology and innovation to enhance the reading experience, from our online platform to interactive author events.
