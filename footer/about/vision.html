Vision & Values

At Pustak, we believe in the transformative power of books and knowledge. Our vision is to create an inclusive literary ecosystem where every individual has access to the books they need to grow, learn, and thrive. Guided by our core values, we strive to make a meaningful impact on our community and beyond.

Our Vision

To be the leading force in democratizing access to knowledge and literature, creating a world where every person can discover, learn, and grow through the power of books.

Inclusive Access

We envision a future where socioeconomic barriers never prevent someone from accessing the books they need. Every individual deserves the opportunity to expand their horizons through literature.

Knowledge Sharing

Our vision extends beyond selling books to creating a vibrant ecosystem where knowledge flows freely, ideas are exchanged, and learning is a shared community experience.

Cultural Bridge

We see ourselves as a bridge connecting diverse cultures, languages, and perspectives through literature, fostering understanding and empathy in our global community.

Our Commitment to Access

We believe that access to books and knowledge should be universal. Our commitment goes beyond words - it's reflected in every initiative we undertake to break down barriers and create opportunities for all.

Affordability

Accessible Pricing

We maintain competitive pricing across our collection and offer regular discounts, payment plans, and special student rates. Our goal is to ensure that cost never becomes a barrier to accessing quality literature and educational materials.

Inclusion

Multilingual Collection

Our diverse collection includes books in Hindi, English, Urdu, and other regional languages, ensuring that language is never a barrier to accessing knowledge. We actively seek out literature that represents diverse voices and perspectives.

Outreach

Community Programs

Through partnerships with schools, NGOs, and community centers, we bring books directly to underserved communities. Our mobile library initiative reaches rural areas where traditional bookstores don't exist.

Access Initiatives

Student Support Program

Special discounts, installment plans, and book buyback programs for students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds.

  • Up to 40% discount on textbooks
  • Interest-free payment plans
  • Book exchange program

Accessibility Features

Ensuring our physical and digital spaces are accessible to people with disabilities.

  • Large print and audio books
  • Wheelchair accessible store
  • Screen reader compatible website

Community Scholarships

Annual scholarships and book grants for deserving students and aspiring writers.

  • Rs. 50,000 in annual scholarships
  • Free books for merit students
  • Author mentorship programs

Our Impact

Over four decades, Pustak has created ripples of positive change in our community. We measure our success not just in books sold, but in lives transformed, minds opened, and dreams realized through the power of literature.

Education

Literacy & Learning

We've supported literacy programs that have benefited over 25,000 individuals, partnering with schools and NGOs to provide books, reading materials, and educational resources to those who need them most.

Authors

Supporting Writers

We've launched the careers of over 150 local authors through our platform, providing them with opportunities to connect with readers, host events, and gain recognition for their work.

Culture

Cultural Renaissance

Through 500+ cultural events, book fairs, and literary festivals, we've fostered a thriving literary culture in Kanpur and surrounding regions, attracting readers from across Uttar Pradesh.

Governance

Pustak operates with transparency, accountability, and ethical practices at its core. Our governance structure ensures that we remain true to our mission while adapting to the evolving needs of our community.

Leadership Structure

Our leadership team combines decades of experience in bookselling, education, and community service. We maintain a flat organizational structure that encourages innovation and quick decision-making.

Ethical Practices

We adhere to strict ethical standards in all our operations, from fair pricing and honest marketing to sustainable business practices and respectful treatment of all stakeholders.

Community Advisory

Our Community Advisory Board includes educators, authors, and local leaders who provide guidance on our programs and ensure we stay connected to community needs.

Governance Framework

Accountability

  • Annual transparency reports
  • Regular community feedback sessions
  • Open book policy on community impact
  • Customer grievance redressal system

Compliance

  • Full compliance with local business laws
  • Adherence to publishing industry standards
  • Regular legal and financial audits
  • Data privacy and security protocols

Sustainability

  • Eco-friendly packaging and operations
  • Support for sustainable publishing
  • Digital-first initiatives to reduce paper waste
  • Partnership with green logistics providers

Stakeholder Engagement

  • Regular author and publisher meetings
  • Customer advisory panels
  • Employee feedback and development
  • Community partnership reviews
